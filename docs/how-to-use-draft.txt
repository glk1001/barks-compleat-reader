How to Use the Barks Reader

Note to use: The Reader should be intuitive enough to understand here is more detail

Concept
    Full knowledge of all Barks stories combined with where stories are in the Fantagraphics volumes

    No censorship
       By providing override files with censorship fixes that are bundled with the app and are read instead of censored pages

    Different navigation options:
        Chronological
        Series
        Categories
        Search:
            by title
            by tag
        Can choose story associated with top or bottom image
        Index:
            alphabetic
            speech bubble index
    Extras
        Intro articles
        Appendix articles
            Rich Tommaso
            Don Ault
            Maggie Thompson
            George Lucas
            Censorship fixes

Configure
    Fanta volumes
        How to name
        What directory to put them in

    Nothing else to change

    Settings
        Your Fantagraphics Volumes directory containing all the Fantagraphics zips
        Go straight to fullscreen on app start
        Go straight to fullscreen for comic reading
        Always read comic in double page mode
        Toggle show title info in top view
        Toggle show title info in bottom fun view
        Toggle first use of reader
        Goto last comic read
        Options to turn off controversial censorship fixes
        Logging level

    Settings Files
        barks-reader.ini
        barks-reader.json
        kivy configs

    Installer
        single exe plus installer zip
        running exe always unzips python files to "pycrucible_payload", then launches app
        first use of app
            unzips files in installer zip
            sets up config in appropriate directory:
                Windows: "C:\Users\User\AppData\Roaming\barks-reader"
                Macos: "~/Library/Application Support"
                Linux: "~/.config/barks-reader"
            sets up support data files (panels and overrides) in appropriate directory:
                Windows: "C:\Users\<UserName>\BarksReader"
                Macos: "~/Library/Application Support"
                Linux: "~/.local/share/barks-reader"
            then runs reader
        subsequent runs of app
            checks that config files are there, then runs reader

Layout
    Main screen top menu
        close app, toggle fullscreen, got back, collapse navigation tree and show bottom fun view, refresh all images, settings
        Can click on menu bar icon to goto the story associated with the icon image

    Can be made full page

    Top view
        Intro
        The Stories (Chronological, series, etc.)
        Search (by title or tag)
        Appendix
        Indexes
        Top background image
            Can click on down arrow to goto the story associated with top image

    Bottom view
        Comic View
            If on comic, then title info plus panel from comic plus title portal button
            Can store last page read and option to turn off controversial censorship fixes
            Partially transparent button top right of bottom view that will turn off title info and portal to show just panel image.
                Can still click through portal button though

        Fun View
            Random selection of panels.
            Panel categories can be controlled with button on top left of fun view
            Can click on up arrow to goto the story associated with top image
            Can click on left or right of panel to move through fun view panel history

    Comic Reader
        Can be made fullscreen
        Simple navigation:
            left click/right click page turns
            top menu
                make visible by clicking top
                options: close, toggle fullscreen, toggle double page, goto start/end, goto page

    Click Me in big letters on title portal
